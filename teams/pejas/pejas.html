TEAM PEJAS


ABOUT THE TEAM

PEJAS is an initiative, intending to support and guide the people of our society about Sustainable Development Goal-16. Its Mission “To develop peaceful and inclusive communities for sustainable development, to provide access to justice for all and to build effective, responsive and inclusive institutions at all levels”. PEJAS helps the students to give an essence of the existing problems in the society and how small acts can bring about a change.


ABOUT THE CAMPAIGN- #CHILLAO SE CHILL

#ChillaoSeChill is a campaign aimed at transforming the current state of society, which is hostile to peace, into a peaceful world. We want to address the underlying causes of human disputes.

Some of the Successful Events:

1. Peace Wall-
The event started on 1st April 2022, Friday at 1:00 PM and lasted till 6th April 2022. Posters of Peace Wall were shared on the social media handles of PEJAS and this fun activity was given full publicity by each team member of PEJAS to create awareness among the people in the campus of IGDTUW and Ambedkar University. The creative team created a Peace Wall banner to further promote the event where we had a Wall of Peace. Students participated voluntarily and wrote their thoughts on paper.Apart from students many higher faculties also took part in the peace wall and wrote their views. Our CSD Core Faculty Coordinator Ar. Kshitij Kumar Sinha and Shikha ma'am also shared their views on the Peace Wall.

2. Unbosom-
Posters of UNBOSOM ('Unboxing' + 'Awesome') were shared on the social media handles of PEJAS and this fun activity was given full publicity by each team member of PEJAS to create awareness among the people in the campus of IGDTUW and Ambedkar University. Students participated voluntarily and Unboxing their thoughts about the most peaceful and felt injustice moment of their life on paper.

3. Anger Management Session-
The online webinar on Anger Management took place on 1st May 2022, Saturday from 12:00 PM to 1:30 PM. Dr. Nisha Khanna was called as the speaker for the webinar. She is a counseling psychologist and has offered counseling to over 30,000 individuals in the last 20 years of her career.Peace can happen only when anger is under control so this was a great event to promote peace. The main objective of this seminar wasto make the students aware of the reasons for their anger and intolerance through games.
