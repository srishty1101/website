TEAM ADIRA


ABOUT THE TEAM

Adira is an initiative by IGDTUW students to bring a change in the society - towards positivity - by doing its part to achieve some sustainable development goals (SDGs). We identify the need for a new approach to gender equality - one that 'ensures access to, and empowerment through, quality education, irrespective of gender or age. Our team focuses on Sustainable Development Goal 4 (Quality education), Sustainable Development Goal 5 (Gender Equality) and Sustainable Development Goal 10(Reduced Inequalities


ABOUT THE CAMPAIGN- SAB PADHENGE AAGE BADHENGE

Our "Sab Padhenge Aage Badhenge" campaign aims at spreading awareness about the need of education for all. Education is our birth right and we believe that everyone shouldve access to it.. In this issue, the different initiatives taken by the team to address the problem and their impact have been described.

Some of the Successful Events:

1. Street Art: Chalk Graffiti
ADIRA organized an event: "Chalk Graffiti" at Taarangana '22, the Annual Cultural fest of IGDTUW, to raise awareness for the sustainable goals they advocate: Gender Equality, Quality Education and Reduced Inequalities. During the event, all interested onlookers were encouraged to express their thoughts using chalk, with the roads as a blank canvas, filled with ideas and thoughts of the students. The team members set the ball rolling by illustrating their ideas on the roads. This inspired students from IGDTUW and other universities, as well as faculty and known "influencers" and YouTubers to join and become a part of this awareness campaign. Throughout the event, the members, including the President and the General Secretary, made the passersby aware of the campaign: "Sab Padhenge Aage Badhenge" and their work with SDGs 4, 5 and 10.

2. Digiteracy
"DIGITERACY", was an insightful session hosted by ADIRA, in collaboration with FINIVESTA . Team FINIVESTA talked about the various government schemes which are meant for economically lower background people and help in opening a bank account . After a brief introduction, they gave comprehensive presentation on the use of digital payment by various UPI apps and also spoke about security in digital payement (advantages and diadvantages). The session was concluded by a successful and interactive Kahoot quiz.


ENDLINE SURVEY

This endline survey was a huge success.. We were able to inform the audience about the importance of education. The audience was highly satisfied and motivated to be the change and ready to be sustainable. The team received lots of support from approximately 350+ participants in the street art, covering 20m of road. Digiteracy was attended by a whooping 600+students The enthusiasm of the students and the positive feedback received made this activity a successful awareness program, they took action towards achieving Sustainable Development Goals.
